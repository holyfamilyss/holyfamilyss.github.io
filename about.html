About Us

Holy Family is a private catholic founded, mixed, day and boarding secondary school. It is an O & A-level offering both Arts and sciences. It was established in 1999 by Rev. Fr. Sylivest Kityo who by then was the parish priest of Holy Family Parish Namayumba. Currently, it is under Kampala Archdiocese. It is found in Holy Family Catholic Parish Namayumba, Naddangira Dinnery, Wakiso Vicariate in Kampala Archdiocese. Politically, it is located in Namayumba cell, Luguzi ward, Namayumba Town council, Wakiso District in Uganda. It is located along Hoima Highway 29 miles from Kampala city. Holy family S.S Namayumba is a school offering secondary education that is aligned with the country’s education policies and legal frameworks. It offers its services bearing in mind that education is a basic human right and aligns its activities with education sector core goal of equipping learners with relevant knowledge and skills necessary for social-economic transformation and development. This is in line with the forth sustainable Development Goal (SDG) which emphasizes inclusive and quality education for all as well as the promotion of lifelong learning.
