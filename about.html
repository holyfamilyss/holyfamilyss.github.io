About Us

Holy Family Secondary School Namayumba is a Catholic-founded mixed day school, established in 1999 under Kampala Archdiocese. We are committed to academic excellence, ICT innovation, and holistic student development. Our institution nurtures learners to grow academically, spiritually, and socially, preparing them to become responsible citizens who live by our guiding motto: “In God We Succeed.”
